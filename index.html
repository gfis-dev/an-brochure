Next Generation Computer Aided Dispatch

Next Generation (NG911/NG999) Emergency Response System (ERS) is the state-of-the-art circling Emergensys NGCAD systems. It focuses to offers enhanced functionalities such as:

  • Enhanced User Interface (UI) for easier navigation and effective handling of emergency calls for dispatchers.
  • Advanced Decision Support Tools to assess incoming situations in real-time and suggest the best resources for dispatch depending on a number of variables, including the emergency's nature, location, and availability.
  • Improved Mobile Functionality enables responders to receive and updates seamlessly incident statutes via their smartphones or tablets.
  • Expanded Reporting Capabilities to improved reporting capabilities which allow agencies to create thorough reports and data on call volumes, response times, and resource usage for more effective performance analysis and decision- making.
  • PCAD (Portable CAD), a mobile edition of the CAD system which is compatible with Windows Operating System, offering more versatility and accessibility for Emergency Service Agencies.

RMMA

Solutions

Products and Service

Resource Management & Monitoring Application

RMMA is an in-house framework conceptualized to describe designing, developing and deploying systems for resource monitoring. Its conception came from projects that were developed for MERS999 CAD system - JBPM Balai Monitoring and Printer Monitoring, and CADSA. It was further refined for a Push to Alert System for Klinik Kesihatan under the Ministry of Health Malaysia.

PUSH TO ALERT (PTA) SYSTEM

Push-to-Alert system (PTA) is a alert solution designed to enhance the responsiveness and efficiency of medical personnels in medical facility such as Klinik Kesihatan (clinic) during emergency situations. PTA allows promptly address emergency request by providing instant alerts directly to personnel mobile phones, with request triggered via PTA IoT devices placed strategically and conveniently at medical facilities entrance.

JBPM BALAIMON & PRINTERMON

Balaimon is a system used to monitor LRT/FRT status and availability at all JBPM Balai Bomba nationwide. It reads and display JBPM fire engine availability information from CAD system, allowing for streamlined management and work operation.

Printermon was developed to manage and monitor printers status at all JBPM Balai Bomba nationwide. Printing call details is a part of JBPM day-to-day work operation. Printermon utilizes a console app that reads and sends data to a central server where a dashboard will update its status to users.

CADSA SYSTEM

CADSA (CAD Synchronizer App) is a connectivity status monitoring and management tool for Agencies (at RC) and PSCCs (at STC). CADSA ensures agencies and PSCC statuses are synchronized at all times to ensure the accuracy of CAD system.
